Privacy Policy for Fidget Fun

Effective Date: October 23, 2025

TL;DR: Fidget Fun collects absolutely no data. Zero. Nothing. We don't track you, analyze you, or store anything about you. It's just a fun app!

1. Information Collection

Fidget Fun does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data of any kind. This includes but is not limited to:

  • No personal identification information
  • No usage statistics or analytics
  • No device information
  • No location data
  • No browsing history
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

2. Data Storage

All app data (such as your drawings, button states, or color selections) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to the developer
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Third-Party Services

Fidget Fun does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. There are no third parties that receive any data from this app.

4. Children's Privacy

Fidget Fun is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Network Activity

Fidget Fun does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any servers. The app works completely offline.

6. Permissions

Fidget Fun only requests the following permissions:

  • Haptic Feedback: To provide vibration/haptic feedback when you interact with the app

No other permissions are requested or used.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (though we have no plans to), we will update this policy and update the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Information

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data about you, there is no data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Object to processing of

You have complete control and privacy by default!

Summary: Fidget Fun is a privacy-first app. We believe your data is yours, and the best way to protect it is to never collect it in the first place.
